About

Lafayette Fashionista was establised in October 1, 2013 by Graciela Guerrero-Reynoso. I invite you into my shop to enjoy and experience consignment shopping full of current fashions at a great price. Offering moderate to high end brands.

"As always, giving back to the community is important to me. We host and participate in many special events throughout the year. Fundraisers for non-profit, Private Parties and Fashion Shows to name a few."

I truly enjoy meeting people from all over the world, all walks of life and for me I consider this a blessing. Whether a first time visitor or a seasoned regular you'll find always a warm and friendly welcome.
