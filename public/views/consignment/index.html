How to Consign

1. How to consign. Please call to schedule an appointment: 925-283-6540.

2. Prepare your items. All items must be in excellent condition (2 years or newer). Cleaned and pressed, ready to be transferred to shop hangers and ready to go out on the floor. (Your hangers will be returned). Accepting current season only.

3. Be prepared to read and sign contract, consignors will be responsible to keep track of expiration date.

We also offer a wide collection of Vintage handbags and hats for a fun look.

There are lot of things to consider before heading into consignment shop. Most important is the presentation and quality of the items you are trying to consign.
